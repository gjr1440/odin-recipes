Red Snapper Cocktail

Home

Description

This red snapper cocktail is a sophisticated spin on the Bloody Mary, blending botanical gin with savory tomato, bright lemon, and a subtle kick of spice. Customize with your favorite garnishes like olives, pickles, or cheese cubes.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon celery salt, or as needed
  • 1 lime wedge
  • 2 cups ice cubes, or as needed
  • 2 fluid ounces London Dry gin
  • 1/2 fluid ounce freshly-squeezed lemon juice
  • 5 dashes Tabasco sauce, or to taste
  • 3 dashes Worcestershire sauce, or to taste
  • 1 (5.5 ounce) can tomato juice
  • 1 celery stick, for garnish

Steps

  1. Sprinkle celery salt onto a plate. Moisten the rim of a highball or pint glass with lime wedge. Press the moistened rim into the salt. Fill the glass with ice.
  2. Add gin, lemon juice, Tabasco sauce, Worcestershire sauce, and tomato juice to the glass. Sprinkle with a pinch of celery salt, if desired, then gently stir to combine. Garnish with lime wedge, celery stick, and any additional toppings.
