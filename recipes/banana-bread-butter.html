Banana Bread Butter

Home

Description

This banana bread butter will make your morning toast taste like yummy, comforting banana bread, and it is so, so easy to make. It only has 2 ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 1 (6.5 ounce) tub cinnamon sugar butter spread
  • 1/2 ripe banana
  • 1 pinch kosher salt

Steps

  1. Add banana, butter spread, and salt to the bowl of a food processor. Whip together until smooth. Return mixture to the tub.
  2. Keep refrigerated until ready to serve.
